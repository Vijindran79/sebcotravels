# Google Business Profile setup — SEBCO Travels

> **This is the single highest-impact thing you can do to get more taxi jobs.**
> A verified, well-optimised Google Business Profile (GBP) is what makes you show up on **Google Maps** and in the local "3-pack" when someone searches "airport taxi near me", "Heathrow transfer Reading", etc. It is **completely free**.
>
> Most family-run UK taxi firms that "get lots of jobs through Google" get them through their GBP, not their website. The website is for closing the lead; the GBP is for *finding* the lead.

---

## Time required

- **Day 1**: 30 minutes to create + submit for verification
- **Day 2–14**: Google posts a verification postcard to your business address (5–14 days). You enter the code online when it arrives.
- **Ongoing**: 15 minutes / week (replying to reviews, posting updates)

---

## Step 1 — Create the profile (10 minutes)

1. Go to **https://business.google.com/create** while signed into the Gmail account you want to manage the business with (use a business Gmail you can keep forever, e.g. `bookings@sebcotravels.co.uk`, NOT a personal one you might lose).
2. **Business name**: `SEBCO Travels`
3. **Category** (CRITICAL — pick the most specific one): start typing and choose **`Taxi service`** as the primary. You can add up to 9 secondary categories later: `Airport shuttle service`, `Chauffeur service`, `Limousine service`, `Transportation service`.
4. **Do you want to add a location customers can visit?** → **No** (you're a service-area business, drivers come to the customer).
5. **Where do you serve customers?** → Add your service areas: e.g. `Reading`, `Slough`, `Wokingham`, `Oxford`, `Heathrow Airport`, `Gatwick Airport`, `Stansted Airport`, `Luton Airport`, `Greater London`. You can list up to 20.
6. **Contact details**:
   - Phone: `+44 7411 113636`
   - Website: `https://sebcotravels.co.uk` (or whatever your real domain is)
7. **Finish and verify.** Choose **Postcard by mail** (this is normally the only option for service-area businesses). Enter the address you use for HMRC / your private hire licence — Google posts a 5-digit code there.
8. **Tick "Get email updates from Google"** so you see review notifications.

---

## Step 2 — While you wait for the postcard (20 minutes)

You can fill in almost everything before verification. Do all of this NOW:

### Profile content

- **Description (750 characters)** — write it once, reuse forever. Example:
  > SEBCO Travels is a family-run premium taxi and airport transfer service based in the South East of England. We operate a chauffeur-driven executive people carrier seating up to 8 passengers with full luggage, free child seats (infant, toddler, booster) and flight tracking on every airport pick-up. All pre-bookings are quoted at a fixed upfront price with no surge and no taximeter — pay only when your journey is complete. Drivers are fully DBS-checked, hire-and-reward insured, and operate 24/7 across Heathrow, Gatwick, Stansted, Luton, the Thames Valley and Greater London. Book online in 60 seconds or call us directly.
- **Opening hours**: `Mon–Sun, 00:00 – 23:59` (i.e. 24/7).
- **From the business** attributes: tick *Family-owned*, *Identifies as women-owned* (if applicable), *Wheelchair accessible* (if your van is), *Online appointments*, *Online estimates*.
- **Services**: add each individually with a short description and a price *from*:
  - `Airport transfer (Heathrow)` — from £35
  - `Airport transfer (Gatwick)` — from £85
  - `Airport transfer (Stansted)` — from £95
  - `Airport transfer (Luton)` — from £75
  - `Family group transfer with child seats` — from £55
  - `Corporate executive chauffeur` — from £60
  - `Wedding / special event` — from £150
  - `Long-distance UK transfer` — from £150
- **Appointment URL** — paste your booking page URL (e.g. `https://sebcotravels.co.uk/#book`). Google shows this as a *Book* button.

### Photos (PRIORITY — listings with 10+ photos get up to 35% more clicks)

Upload all of these. Use your phone in good daylight; resolution ≥ 720 × 720.

- **Cover photo**: front 3/4 of your van, gold-hour lighting
- **Logo / Profile photo**: clean SEBCO logo on solid background
- 3 × **exterior** photos of the van (front, side, rear)
- 3 × **interior** photos (driver/passenger seats, middle row, boot/luggage area, child seat fitted)
- 2 × **at-work** photos (van at Heathrow drop-off, you in chauffeur attire opening the door for a family)
- 2 × **detail** photos (clean leather seat, complimentary water bottle, charger socket)
- **One short video** (≤ 30 seconds) showing the van, the interior space, and a happy goodbye wave. Phone vertical video is fine.

> Refresh photos every 2–3 months. Google's algorithm favours active profiles.

## Step 3 — When the postcard arrives (5 minutes)

1. Sign in to **https://business.google.com**.
2. Click *Verify now* and enter the 5-digit code from the postcard.
3. The listing becomes live within ~24 hours.

---

## Step 4 — Get your first reviews (the most important step)

Reviews are the #1 factor in Google's local ranking. A new profile with 0 reviews will be invisible no matter how good the photos are.

### The 48-hour push

1. Make a list of your **last 20 happy customers** (phone book, WhatsApp history, email).
2. Get your **review short-link**: in your GBP dashboard → *Get more reviews* → copy the link (looks like `https://g.page/r/CXXXX/review`).
3. Send each customer a personal message (text / WhatsApp). Template:
   > Hi [Name], it was great driving you to Heathrow last week — I hope the trip went well! We've just launched on Google and your honest review would really help our family business grow. It takes about 30 seconds: **[paste link]**. Thank you! — Sebastian, SEBCO Travels

### Ongoing

- After **every** completed trip, hand the customer a small card with the review link as a QR code. (Use https://www.qr-code-generator.com — free.)
- Reply to **every** review within 24 hours — even 5-star ones. Google rewards profiles with high reply rates.
- Aim for **25 reviews in 90 days**. After that the listing starts pulling its weight.

### Reply templates

- **5★**: "Thank you so much, [Name]! It was a pleasure driving you and the family. Safe onward travels — see you again soon. — Sebastian"
- **4★** (anything constructive): "Thank you for the kind words and for the feedback on [the thing they mentioned]. We've taken it on board and will [specific fix]. — Sebastian, SEBCO Travels"
- **1–3★**: Reply publicly, calmly, briefly, **then take it offline**: "Thank you for letting us know — this isn't the standard we hold ourselves to. I'd like to make it right; please WhatsApp me on +44 7411 113636 so I can hear the full story personally. — Sebastian, SEBCO Travels"

---

## Step 5 — Weekly maintenance (15 minutes / week)

- **Post an Update** every Monday: a new photo, a route ("Just dropped a family at Gatwick Terminal North — see you on your return Sunday!"), or a seasonal offer. Posts only show for 7 days, so this needs to be regular.
- **Answer Q&A** if anyone posts a question (you'll get email alerts).
- **Add a new photo** every 2 weeks (Google measures freshness).
- **Add a new Service** any time you start offering something new (school runs, hen-do transport, etc.).

---

## Step 6 — Free extras that compound the GBP

- **Bing Places** — clone of GBP for Microsoft / Bing / Yahoo / DuckDuckGo. Same data. Free. 5 minutes. **https://www.bingplaces.com**
- **Apple Business Connect** — Apple Maps / Spotlight / Siri. Free. 5 minutes. **https://businessconnect.apple.com**
- **Yell** — UK's biggest free directory. Free basic listing. **https://www.yell.com**
- **Trustpilot** — free profile, request reviews after each trip. **https://business.trustpilot.com**
- **Yelp UK** — fewer UK users but still indexed by Google. **https://biz.yelp.co.uk**

All five take a combined ~30 minutes. Each one is a small extra trickle of leads, and Google sees the consistent name/address/phone across them — which boosts your GBP ranking further.
